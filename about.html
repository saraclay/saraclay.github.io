Design × Audience
I've been fascinated with the intersection of design, marketing and branding for as long as I can remember. As John Maeda's DesignIn.Tech report explains, startups have led the way in showing the growing importance of uniformity in visual identity and branding.

Currently at Microsoft, I've been part of the startup-like transformation of our open source identity. I've led our presence at third-party open source events, created various collateral showcasing our open source story and have worked with our startup and gaming teams to drive increased Azure usage through these audiences.

Having worked in mobile gaming before, I also continue to be fascinated by the mobile ecosystem and stay close to mobile news through the AppBoy, App Annie and Benedict Evans' blogs and more.
